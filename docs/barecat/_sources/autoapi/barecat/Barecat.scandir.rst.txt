scandir
=======

.. py:method:: barecat.Barecat.scandir(path)

   Iterate over all files and sub directories, as BarecatFileInfo or BarecatDirInfo
   objects.

   :param path: Path to the directory within the archive.

   :returns: An iterator over BarecatFileInfo or BarecatDirInfo objects.
